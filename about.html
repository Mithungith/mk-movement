About Us

WELCOME

Pacific Physiotherapy is one of the best wellness clinics in Ontario, Canada. We stand out for offering a holistic approach to health and wellness. Our team solely comprises some of the best experts from across the globe. Together they have blended in a variety of practices, which has allowed us to integrate various facilities that offer excellent health and wellness to all our clients. Our experts excel at offering a wide range of health and wellness treatments, including acupuncture, orthopedic physiotherapy, chiropractic, shockwave therapy, massage therapy, joint mobilization, and cold laser. Some of our other services include offering therapeutic exercise sessions, myofascial release, ergonomic training, balance training, heat/cryotherapy, and electrical stimulation.
